DETAILS

The autocompounder vaults on this page are used for those investors that prefer to take a back seat. They automatically compound the users rewards either every 2 hours (through a gelato script) or whenever another user claims the bounty using the Harvest function.

Upon each compound these vaults take a 4% performance fee from the claimed rewards for that compound and use 100% of this to buy back and burn the TOAST token; which is why you will see lots of tiny buys on TOAST's dexscreener chart.

NB: If there is any withdrawal tax for pools that these vaults are associated with you will have to pay this upon withdrawing your rewards.
